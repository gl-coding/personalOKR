# Privacy Policy - Personal OKR

*Last updated: March 29, 2025*

## Overview

Personal OKR ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection

**Personal OKR does not collect any personal data.** The App operates entirely offline and does not transmit any information to external servers.

## Data Storage

All data you create in the App — including OKRs, objectives, key results, reminders, check-ins, and weekly reports — is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Notifications

The App uses local notifications to deliver reminders you configure. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the Settings screen within the App. Uninstalling the App will also remove all stored data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
