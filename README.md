# Personal OKR - Support

## About

Personal OKR is a productivity app that helps you plan and track your personal goals using the OKR (Objectives and Key Results) methodology. All data is stored locally on your device.

## Features

- Create and manage OKRs with Objectives and Key Results
- Daily check-in tracking with streaks and statistics
- Smart reminders (one-time, daily, weekdays, custom)
- Weekly report recording
- Outline editor and mind map visualization
- Data export and import

## FAQ

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

**Q: Can I back up my data?**
A: Yes. Use the export feature to save your OKR data, and import it on another device.

**Q: How do reminders work?**
A: Reminders use local notifications on your device. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

**Q: Does this app require an internet connection?**
A: No. The app works completely offline.

## Privacy

Personal OKR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information remains on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details.
